Robertify

A discord music bot with a multitude of features that will fit your liking!

Invite Learn More

Favourite Tracks

Save a track as one of your favourites! Once saved, you can add this track to the queue any time you'd like! All it takes is one click.

Themes

Express your personality using the all new themes command! There are now 9 colour options for you to choose from to truly express and bring life to your music listening experience.

An interactive player!

Check out our fancy interactive player! Whenever you get too tired of using our boring old commands, you can use this easy access channel to control your music. In this channel you can also requests songs without running a single command. All you have to do is type the name of the song or provide the link.

Sources Galore!

There are many sources for you to choose from! The biggest, of course, being Spotify, Deezer, YouTube and SoundCloud. We also support streams from both YouTube and Twitch, Vimeo, HTTP Audio Files, Beam and even local audio files (MP3, OGG, WAV, M4A, FLAC).

Command Overload!

Robertify has been outfitted with tons of commands for you to truly have an immersive experience with the bot and your music. There are four categories: Management, Music, Miscellaneous and Utility. So don't be shy, there's a whole world out there waiting for you!

Slash Commands!

Too many syntaxes for you to remember? No problem, we have slash commands for you. No idea what slash commands are? Well they are these wonderful new tools that Discord implemented for you to easily interact with our bot. Most of our commands have also been listed as slash commands and for commands that require a syntax, you're told exactly what to put. How cool is that?!

Need Help?

You can always join our support server to ask us questions about anything. You can also suggest new features, report bugs, talk about music and so much more. Don't be afraid to reach out, we'd love to help you!
